Mango Smoothie

Description:

This fresh drink will help you with the warm summer time and light your tastebuds up!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Mangoes
  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Yogurt

Steps to Make this Delicious Delight:

  1. Take all the ingredients in a Mixer Jar
  2. Convert the ingredients into a very smooth paste.
  3. That's It! Enjoy your smoothie with a straw! You can add some small mango cubes on the drink to make it look cool!
