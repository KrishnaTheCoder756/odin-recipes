Indian Style Veg Pakoda

Description:

This indian style crunchy-munchy recipe with pudina herbs and Indian spices will light up your mouth with its taste. You can't just help yourself with this!

Ingredients:

  • Gram Flour
  • Rice Flour
  • Ajwain (Carom Seeds)
  • Garam Masala
  • Pudina (Mint) Leaves
  • Onions
  • Cabbage
  • French beans
  • Bell Peppers
  • Carrots
  • A few green chillies
  • Ginger Garlic Paste

Steps to Make this Delicious Delight:

  1. Take 1 cup shredded cabbage, 1 medium onion sliced, 1 medium carrot, 4 to 5 french beans julienned (Don't forget to chop off the bean inside french beans)1/4 cup capsicum.

  2. Add the pudina leaves in the veggies. Also add 1-2 chopped green chillies, 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste.

  3. Now Add 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon garam masala and half teaspoon salt.

  4. Mix them together and squeeze the veggies to remove some moisture. Set aside for 10 mins.

  5. Add 1/2 cup gram flour (besan), 1/2 teaspoon ajwain and 1/4 cup rice flour.

  6. Mix them together to form a dough. if your mixture is too dry, sprinkle some water, but make sure not to pour a lot of water as we want a stiff dough. Taste the dough and add more garam masala, salt, chillies if required.

  7. Heat oil in a pan in medium heat. Flatten your doughs a little with your hands (Don't shape them to patties). Drop them in hot oil. Do not drop in lumps

  8. Wait for 1 to 2 mins, stir and fry them. Fry them until light golden. Drain the oil and take them out.

  9. Tada! Your Indian Style crunchy vegetable pakodas are ready!
